Bird Song Classification

This website utilizes an advanced model to accurately predict bird species based on their songs. The model is proficient in identifying species such as the American Robin, Bewick's Wren, Northern Cardinal, Northern Mockingbird, and the Song Sparrow. It was meticulously trained using a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) on a comprehensive public dataset sourced from https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/vinayshanbhag/bird-song-data-set and https://www.xeno-canto.org/, ensuring robust and reliable predictions.

Upload an audio file (in .wav format) and click the "Upload" button to classify the bird according to its song. If the prediction does not have at least a confidence level of 85%, the class will not be determined.
